Hi.

I'm Gauri Ramesh and I'm a second-year student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln majoring in Computer Science, with minors in Math and Business. I made this website from scratch to learn web dev/design.

My two favorite things are learning and thinking. In my free time, I like to code, mess around with my camera, play the piano, write, read books that make me think, and spend time with my loved ones.

People oftentimes find me outside on long walks or at coffee shops studying, coding, and listening to open mic nights or Hamilton.

I aspire to be a full stack engineer at a company that strives to make a difference.

I am really passionate about making people the best versions of themselves as they can be. As part of my long term career plans, I want to be a driving force in closing the gender gap in technology. Along with being a developer, I also would love to teach and work with kids to get them excited about technology and what they can do with it in the future.

I strive to be action-oriented, and I believe that dreaming it should mean doing it.

Thanks for stopping by. Navigate the icons to learn more about what I do while the Earth spins.
